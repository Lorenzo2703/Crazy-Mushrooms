Charity

Our team actively wants to give its contribute in the fight against global warming. Our very logo is a tree, representing the will to reach a greener economy, respecting the environment. Our mushrooms have been created to spread positivity and fun, but also to help the nature (their house). For these reasons we have decided that we will plant a tree for every mushroom sold, and the more we grow the more trees will be planted on every sale. We will be teaming up with TeamTree. The buyers shall receive a notification that certificates the completed process.
